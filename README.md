Cuban Neuroscience Center

This is a dataset containing 173 EEGs including 86 healthy subjects and 87 COVID patients
EEG was acquired using a 10-20 standard montage. Resting EEG was recorded for 8 minutes in all participants with eyes closed. Afterwards, 
it was also recorded 2 minutes of alternating closed and open eyes, followed by 2 minutes of recovery.
